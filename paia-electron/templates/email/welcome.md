Subject: Welcome to PAiA — three things to try first

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for installing PAiA. It's a small floating ball in your screen
corner; click it and you're in a chat panel that runs on your own
hardware.

Three things worth trying in your first session:

1. **Press Ctrl/⌘+K** — command palette. Jump to any thread, slash
   command, or action without leaving the keyboard.

2. **Select text anywhere, then Ctrl+Alt+Q** — quick actions popup.
   Summarize, explain, fix grammar, translate, change tone. Eight
   one-click prompts.

3. **Drop a PDF or paste an image into the chat box** — the composer
   handles both. Vision-capable models will see the image; text files
   get ingested into a knowledge collection you can keep citing.

Your trial of Pro runs for 14 days — every feature unlocked. When it
ends, the Free tier keeps working; you just lose agent mode, cloud
providers, canvas, and a few others. You can always come back.

real inbox.
