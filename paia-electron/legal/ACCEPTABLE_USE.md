# PAiA Acceptable Use Policy (TEMPLATE)

> ⚠️ Template — not lawyer-reviewed.

**VERSION:** 0.1-draft
**Effective:** `[DATE]`

You may not use PAiA to, or assist anyone else in using PAiA to:

1. **Violate law.** Do anything illegal in your jurisdiction or the
   jurisdiction of anyone you're interacting with (including, where
   applicable, export-control rules on software and AI models).
2. **Infringe rights.** Infringe copyright, trademark, trade secret,
   or other intellectual property rights; violate publicity, privacy,
   or moral rights.
3. **Harm systems.** Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of any
   system or network without authorization; bypass or circumvent
   security or authentication measures; send malware, viruses, or
   destructive code via any tool PAiA provides.
4. **Harm people.**
   - Generate or distribute sexual content involving minors (including
     "lolicon" / "shota" animated or drawn content — same rule).
   - Generate content designed to facilitate stalking, harassment, or
     non-consensual intimate imagery.
   - Generate content designed to facilitate violent extremism or
     specific physical harm to people.
   - Generate content designed to deceive for election interference.
5. **Weaponize the agent.** Use the agent / browser-use / remote-browser
   tools to perform unauthorized actions on systems you don't have
   permission to act on. The approval gates are meant for you; don't
   approve actions that aren't within your authority.
6. **Abuse scale.** Use the LAN companion or local API server to
   relay requests from third parties in a way that circumvents their
   rate limits, their terms of service, or their billing with cloud
   LLM providers.
7. **Circumvent features.** Remove, obscure, or tamper with the PII
   redaction pipeline, the classroom-policy enforcement, the license
   verification, or the signing hooks.
8. **Claim endorsement.** Imply that PAiA, its publisher, or its
   contributors endorse your use of the software for any specific
   purpose, unless expressly authorized in writing.
9. **Impersonate.** Use PAiA to impersonate a person or entity you
   aren't authorized to represent (including using the voice /
   image-gen tools to produce likenesses of real people without
   consent).

Violation of this Policy may result in immediate suspension or
termination of your licence without refund. Egregious or criminal
conduct may also be reported to law enforcement.

If you see something that violates this Policy, please report it to
