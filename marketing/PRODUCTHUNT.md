## How to set up the launch

1. **2 weeks before:** ask a top hunter (someone with 5K+ followers who has hunted privacy / dev tools / Mac apps) to schedule your launch. The hunter gets credit, you get distribution.
2. **1 week before:** prepare your media kit:
   - 5+ screenshots (the ball, the chat panel, settings, the RAG flow, the onboarding wizard)
   - 1 product video (60 seconds, Loom is fine)
   - GIF of the core interaction (click ball → panel expands → type → response streams)
3. **Day of:** maker comment goes up at 12:01am Pacific time. You stay online for the next 12 hours.
4. **First 4 hours:** reply to every comment. Vote on every comment that's positive. Politely engage with negative ones.
5. **End of day:** thank the community in a follow-up comment.

Make sure your maker profile has:
- A real photo

## What to do if you launch and traffic is low

It happens. Two things:

1. Don't panic-spam. The launch lives on for the rest of the week and recovers if your maker comment is good and the product is good.
2. The next day, post a "what I learned" follow-up on Twitter. PH launches that look like flops sometimes get a second wind from social.
