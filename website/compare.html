Pick the one that fits you

Pick PAiA when

  • You care about your chats staying on your device.
  • You want to run local open-weight models (Llama, Qwen, DeepSeek).
  • You already have an OpenAI / Anthropic / OpenRouter key and don't want a second subscription.
  • You want to automate smart-home, Gmail, Calendar, GitHub via one assistant.
  • You teach a computer lab and need actual enforcement, not just polite suggestions.
  • You want to build plugins and drive the tool from scripts.
  • You want a single agent that owns your browser, clipboard, screen, and files — with approval gates.

Pick something else when

  • You need a first-party mobile app with offline iCloud sync (use ChatGPT or Claude).
  • You want the absolute cutting edge of the flagship model's multimodal features the day they ship — PAiA catches up in days, not hours.
  • Your whole life is already in Raycast and you want everything in one launcher.
  • You don't want to run Ollama or bring an API key. PAiA works best when you do one of the two.
