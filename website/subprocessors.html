Subprocessors

Last updated: [DATE].

A "subprocessor" here means a third party we engage to deliver the paid tiers of PAiA and which may touch limited customer data. It does not include third parties you configure yourself (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, GitHub, your sync host, your Sentry DSN, your feedback collector) — in those cases your relationship is directly with that provider under their own terms.

Name Purpose Data touched Region
[Billing processor — Paddle / LemonSqueezy / Stripe] Payment + billing Name, email, country, payment-method metadata, order history US / EU
[Email delivery — Resend / Postmark / SendGrid] Transactional email (license keys, receipts, support) Name, email, license key US / EU
[License host — Fly.io / Railway / own infra] Accepting license-issuance webhooks from the billing processor Name, email, license key, payload signatures User-specified region
[Analytics — Plausible / self-hosted] Opt-in product analytics (strictly off by default) Anonymous install UUID, event names from a property whitelist EU
[Crash reporter — self-hosted GlitchTip / Sentry Cloud] Opt-in crash telemetry (strictly off by default) Stack traces, PAiA version, redacted breadcrumbs EU
GitHub Release distribution + auto-update check Install IP + user-agent (logged by GitHub) US

Adding or removing subprocessors

For Team-tier customers on a signed DPA, we notify via email at least 30 days before engaging a new subprocessor. You may reasonably object on data-protection grounds; if we cannot resolve the objection, you may terminate the affected portion of the Service.
